How does this work?

Simply input your relation schema and any given functional dependencies (in the correct format), press submit, and voila! ClozureDB will calculate as much useful information as it can and output it below.

What information will ClozureDB give me?

As of right now, ClozureDB is focused primarily on calculating the closures, keys, and superkeys of your schema. In the future (hopefully), it will provide much more information such as your schema's normal form!

Attribute Closures
